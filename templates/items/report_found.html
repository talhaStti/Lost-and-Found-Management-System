Tips for Handling Found Items

Don't Open Private Items

Respect privacy by not opening wallets, bags, or electronics unless necessary to identify the owner.

Note the Time & Place

Be specific about where and when you found the item.

Handle with Care

Keep items in the same condition you found them to avoid damage.

Add a Photo

A clear image of the item will greatly help in matching it with its owner.
